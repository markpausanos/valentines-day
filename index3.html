Conquer me? Conquer you.
Dear Charisse,

I hope this letter finds you well today, and it is the rightful day where people express their
love to whom they admire. You will have become a subject to my letters because of that
one fateful moment of truth that had made me defenseless. Think of it as an art of
vanquishing, I have long planned an offensive to your heart so that I conquer it first
exquisitely. I have made several preparations so that I will have great chances of success.
You, however, conquered me first. I did not prepare for a defensive plan. I was very
oblivious with what to do next, as I might step on a landmine with my words. But then
unexpectedly, this mind planned out a counter-offensive in three days. It had to be done.
A fateful decision to which I cherish to this day, and so I hope you do too. I hope that we
now will move forward to developing our relationship and ourselves. I have done quite
ample research on how to become a better lover for you. It sounds funny but it is how my
thoughts work. I know we will face challenges in the future, right? No matter. You stay
adorable, admirable, and ambitious. I hope to face those challenge with you by my side.

Lovingly yours,
Mark
